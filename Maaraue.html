Outdoor pool

>> read more >> continue to culture page The Maaraue outdoor pool welcomes many bathers, both big and small. It's clear: Here, maximum stands for true size. The Maaraue invites you to indulge in a generous 50,000 square meters of sunbathing areas, has numerous play and sports areas for active guests, and offers immense water fun with a 50-meter swimming pool, diving bay, 75-meter non-swimmer pool, and a spacious paddling area. What's more, the Maaraue outdoor pool offers barrier-free access.
